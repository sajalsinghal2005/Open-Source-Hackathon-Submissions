## Project Details

**Project Title:**  
LinkUp AI

**Project Description:**  
LinkUp AI is an AI-powered career ecosystem designed to help students and freshers streamline their job search journey. The platform provides AI-powered resume analysis, ATS score evaluation, job matching, interview preparation, resume building, application tracking, and a LinkedIn-style community feed. It solves the problem of using multiple disconnected platforms by bringing job discovery, resume optimization, interview preparation, and professional networking into one unified platform.

**Tech Stack Used:**  
React, TypeScript, Firebase, Tailwind CSS, Gemini AI, RapidAPI, Cloudinary, EmailJS
